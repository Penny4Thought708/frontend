DIY Flooring Guide

Smart ideas, helpful tips, and what not to do.

STEP 1: Subflooring 101

Step 1: Clean Any Debris

Remove dust, nails, and old adhesive with a broom or vacuum. A clean surface ensures proper bonding.

Step 2: Patch or Replace

Inspect for water damage. Replace soft or moldy sections with new plywood or OSB panels.

Step 3: Level the Surface

Apply a leveling compound with a notched trowel to eliminate dips and bumps. Let it cure fully.

Step 4: Install Subfloor Panels

Lay plywood sheets perpendicular to joists. Secure with screws every 6–8 inches along edges and field.

Tips for Success

  • ✅ Measure twice, cut once.
  • ✅ Acclimate flooring materials before installation.
  • ✅ Use spacers for even gaps.
  • ✅ Clean and level the subfloor first.

What Not to Do

  • ❌ Don’t skip underlayment—it reduces noise and moisture.
  • ❌ Don’t rush—plan layout before starting.
  • ❌ Don’t ignore expansion gaps—flooring needs room to breathe.
  • ❌ Don’t use wood flooring in damp areas.
